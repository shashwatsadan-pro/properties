Builder Agreement Review & Protection

Before you sign a Builder-Buyer Agreement, our legal team ensures the document is fair, compliant, and protects your financial and timeline interests. This service is crucial for avoiding future legal hurdles.

How We Provide This Expert Service

  • Meticulous Review: We analyze every clause, penalty, delivery date, and hidden cost in the agreement provided by the builder.
  • Risk Mitigation: We identify and highlight clauses that unduly favor the builder, advising you on necessary amendments to secure your rights.
  • Legal Compliance: We verify that the agreement adheres to all current real estate laws and regulations (e.g., RERA where applicable).
  • Negotiation Support: We help you negotiate better terms with the builder based on legal grounds, ensuring maximum protection for your investment.

Details Required to Start Your Review

To conduct a thorough review and provide accurate advice, please prepare the following documents:

  • The complete Draft Builder-Buyer Agreement (unsigned).
  • Booking Receipt and all payment proofs made to date.
  • The Payment Schedule or plan agreed upon.
  • Any written correspondence (emails, letters) with the builder regarding terms.

Disputed Property Resolution: Legal Certainty

A Dispute is any legal claim or contest regarding the ownership, title, boundary, or rights associated with a property. Our resolution service aims to clear titles and settle conflicts efficiently, securing your investment.

How We Resolve Complex Disputes

  • Case Assessment: Comprehensive review of all historical records, deeds, and claims to establish the property's legal history.
  • Strategic Litigation: Developing and executing a tailored legal strategy, representing you in court or through arbitration.
  • Documentation & Title Clearance: Working directly with revenue departments and legal bodies to finalize clear, undisputed titles.
  • Mediation & Settlement: Negotiating out-of-court settlements where possible to save time and resources.

Documents Required for Dispute Review

Please provide every document related to the property and the dispute:

  • All Sale Deeds, Gift Deeds, or Succession Certificates.
  • Copies of Mutation/Revenue Records (Khata/Khasra).
  • Any Court Orders, Notices, or Legal Filings from the opposing party.
  • Property Tax Receipts and Encumbrance Certificates.
